About

I’m a builder by instinct, whether it’s websites, custom PCs, or tools that don’t exist yet but should. I like creating things that are useful, intentional, and genuinely enjoyable for real people to interact with.

My background isn’t linear — My education is in environmental science and biology, I've spent over a decade on a local farm, and now operate as both a Medical Assistant and the go-to IT problem-solver inside a private medical practice. It’s made me adaptable, fast-learning, and deeply aware of how technology actually serves (or fails) humans.

I don’t *always* just code for the sake of code. My focus is on solving real problems, designing with empathy, and making digital spaces that feel calm, functional, and alive. I care about solving real problems and creating experiences that can genuinely improve someone’s day.
