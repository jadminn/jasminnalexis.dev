About Me

Hello World!

My name is Jasminn Alexis, Creator, Designer and Founder of Symbiospaces LLC.

I consider myself a Jazz of all trades. I spent my formative years employed at an apple orchard in Chester, NH. I got a bachelors degree of science in environmental science with a minor in biology, which took me to Africa to study sharks, and Costa Rica to study ecosystems. Now I work part-time as a medical assistant and part-time contracting as Website Support while I grow my business.

I've created this site to showcase my work for others, as well as keep track of everything I'm doing for myself. Here you will find my current and past client projects, personal creations, artwork, and certificates. This site will continue to change and grow over the next few months as I take on and document more projects. If you would like to work together please do not hesitate to reach out.

Happy Coding!

~ Jasminn Alexis
